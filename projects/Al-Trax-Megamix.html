Al-Trax Megamix

Details

Featuring several artists, including me, This album is a megacollab between members of the label. Initially, this was an idea by me, but I wanted us to make just one song by multiple musicians, but then we decided it would be cool to have us remix each others' works instead. The whole process took some time, but in the end it turned out cool and we all managed to squeeze out neat ideas here and there

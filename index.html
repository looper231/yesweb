About me

Started fisrt in 2021, I became interested in music on Trackers such as: Protracker, Fasttracker 2 and Impulse tracker. Besides some covers and silly note spamming which was never really released anywhere. In 2022, Jeansowaty inspired me to remix one of his tracks, that ended up kickstarting my progress. I kept doing various kinds of music on trackers, including remixes and covers, until I discovered FL Studio and learned it bit by bit. Right now, I am more focused on producing tunes on FL Studio now, but I occasionally return to trackers as well.
